About Le Blanc

Absolute pureness, subtle aroma. This oil is best described as the mystique behind exceptional cooking.

Once poured onto a cooking surface or added in a salad, its golden veil is so transparent you can hardly say it is there. But it is – to bring into your kitchen an overwhelming experience of cooking with difference, to be part of a culinary masterpiece.

When it comes to refined sunflower oil, Le Blanc equals perfection.

What makes Le Blanc so special?

01. State-of-the-art refining
01

The unique refining system maximizes the retention of beneficial nutrients in a seed. As a result Le Blanc becomes a concentrated source of healthy nutrients meeting longestablished standards of quality food.

02. The Gift of the Sun
02

We carefully select the sunflower seeds, grown entirely on our lands.

We maintain diligent quality control throughout the entire production. Our goal is to have the seeds grown, harvested and blended according to the highest standards of the industry.

Our signature Le Blanc blend is a celebration of sunlight pouring its warmth into a bottle. We examine the kernels from every angle to process the best of them and bottle the oil under Le Blanc brand – a signature product for demanding customers who choose top quality ingredients.

03. Perfect contents in a perfect shape
03

Well-balanced design of a Le Blanc bottle is brought to you by our creative team. Convenient, easy to hold silhouette is a classic with a stylish twist. Le Blanc oil is available in bottles of different volumes. It will find its place by the stovetop for smooth cooking or at the table to finish a dish with a dash of sunflower oil.

Elegant style of a bottle along with a subtle aroma of sunkissed oil – is a secret behind Le Blanc perfection.

04. Better than perfect
04

So, a bottle of Le Blanc has finally arrived at your kitchen. Hold it for a while.

It was designed to combine contemporary style with ease of control. Pour Le Blanc oil in a frying pan and enjoy your cooking!

Our inspiration comes from exceptional French cuisine — natural flavours, fresh ingredients, effortless chic à la française — Le Blanc is here to provide you with precise balance of quality and simplicity.
